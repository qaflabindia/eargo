# Workflows

Steps stacked into workflows. Each numbered step is narrated in plain
English and delegated to the persona named in parentheses; the `Policies:`
line attaches governing policies from policy.md by name.

## Underwriting Workflow

1. Band the applicant's credit profile. (Credit Risk Guru)
2. Assign a risk grade from the banded profile. (Credit Risk Guru)
3. Decide approve or decline against the grade. (Credit Risk Guru)
4. Write the customer note announcing the decision. (Customer Advocate)

Policies: Loan Amount Cap

### Deliverable

The decision as structured facts, extracted from the prose and judged
against these meanings before they are delivered:

- decision: exactly one of approve or decline
- risk grade: the letter grade from A to E the decision rests on
- decisive factor: the single factor named as decisive, in a short phrase
